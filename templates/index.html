About Chronic Kidney Disease

Understanding CKD

Chronic Kidney Disease affects millions worldwide. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment.

Chronic

A condition that develops slowly over time and can persist for months or years, requiring ongoing management and monitoring.

Kidney

Vital organs that filter blood, remove waste products, and maintain fluid and electrolyte balance in the body.

Disease

An abnormal condition that affects kidney function, potentially leading to serious health complications if left untreated.

Kidney Care

Holistic Approach to Kidney Health

Effective kidney care involves comprehensive monitoring, lifestyle modifications, and early intervention strategies to maintain optimal kidney function.

Why is CKD Important?

CKD is a serious condition affecting over 7.8 million people in India alone. Many remain undiagnosed, making early detection crucial for preventing kidney failure.

How is CKD Diagnosed?

Diagnosis involves blood tests, urine analysis, and imaging studies to assess kidney function and identify underlying conditions.

Understanding Risk Factors

Chronic Kidney Disease can develop due to various factors. Understanding these risk factors helps in prevention and early detection.

  • Diabetes
  • Hypertension
  • Glomerulonephritis
  • Polycystic Kidney Disease
  • Obstructions
  • Autoimmune Diseases
  • Certain Medications
  • Family History
  • Age Factor
  • Smoking
